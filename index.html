The year is 2004 B.E. (Bird Era), and the election for the next Bird President is in full swing. The citizens of UBSBA (United Bird States of Bird America) are facing an onslaught of misinformation and propaganda masquerading as legitimate news.

Your job is to figure out how to categorize the news you find armed with nothing but your wits and a search bar. By plugging keywords in, you'll uncover information about the claims made and the people who made them.
